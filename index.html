About

Hello! I am a Ph.D. student at the DMIS Lab at Korea University advised by Jaewoo Kang. My research area is based on natural language processing (NLP) and deep learning. Specifically, I am interested in enabling and enhancing human-machine conversation in various scenarios. Currently, my research focuses on investigating (1) how questions are asked and answered [SimSeek, ExCorD], and (2) how to effectively search for relevant information [Conv-Shortcut] in conversational QA. I also have a broad interest in expanding it to multimodal tasks [CheXOFA] or real-world applications.

I previously interned at Microsoft Research Asia (Mentor: Eric Chang) and Naver AI Lab (Mentor: Sungdong Kim).
